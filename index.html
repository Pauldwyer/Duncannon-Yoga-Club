Our Story

Duncannon Yoga Club was founded by Denise Dwyer in 2016 encouraged by her husband to get somewhere of her own after years of teaching on Duncannon and in the local community hall. It was her own little private quiet space when she opened and unexpectedly the studio community has been building ever since. It has continued to grow into a local haven where people can come to escape the craziness of their everyday lives. But staying true to her roots she still teaches a class every Sunday morning 9am on the beach !

what yoga do we teach ??

    Yin Yoga

  • Slow-paced style of yoga as exercise
  • Incorporates principles of traditional Chinese medicine
  • Asanas (postures) that are held for longer periods of time than in other styles.

    Hatha Yoga

  • Most common form of yoga taught in the West today.
  • Uses physical techniques to preserve and channel the vital force or energy.

    Gentle Yoga / Meditation

  • Gentler style of Hatha Yoga
  • Performed at slower pace
  • Extended time for meditation, breathwork and relaxation
